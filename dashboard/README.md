## Dashboard Overview

### The dashboard has the following visuals:
### Three card visuals for 'Total Revenue' which is a sum of the Sale_amount, 'Average Revenue' which is the average of the Sales_amount and 'Total Suppliers'
 ### Pie chart: showing the revenue across the 3 categories
 ### Line chart: showing the reveue/sales generated over time (last quarter 2017 -November to third quarter -August 2018)
 ### Clustered column chart showing Revenue across subcategory
###  Table visual showing Supplier, Category and Total Sales
###  Slicer: Subcategory
 ### Slicer: year
